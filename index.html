What is a Smart Grid?

Smart grids are the result of the evolution of electric power systems. They are evolved networks that manage electricity demand in a sustainable, reliable and economical way. They are characterized by an advanced infrastructure adapted to facilitate the integration of new components into the smart grid.

INNOVATIVE SYSTEMS

With the new smart bidirectional meters, all protection and control divices and the wide use of the internet the grid has become intelligent (smart).

A smart grid is an electrical grid that uses information and communications technology to gather and act on information about the behaviours of suppliers and consumers, in an automated fashion to improve the efficiency, reliability, economics, and sustain ability of the production and distribution of electricity.
